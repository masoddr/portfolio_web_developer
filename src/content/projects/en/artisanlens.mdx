ArtisanLens is a portfolio platform specifically designed for professional photographers. The project aimed to create a space where photographers can showcase their work in an elegant and professional manner.

The platform was developed with React and Node.js, offering a smooth user experience and advanced portfolio management features.

Key project features:
- Customizable galleries
- Client management system
- Integrated online store
- Visit statistics
